The Best Movies According To Me

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

KGF Chapter 3

Rocky successfully rises as the leader and saviour of the people of the Kolar Gold Fields. But, in his goal to fulfil his mother's wishes, Rocky must tackle Adheera, Inayat Khalil and Ramika Sen.

Interstellar

When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future, a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, is tasked to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers, to find a new planet for humans.

The Hobbit Series

The Hobbit is a series of three epic high fantasy adventure films directed by Peter Jackson. The films are subtitled An Unexpected Journey, The Desolation of Smaug, and The Battle of the Five Armies.
